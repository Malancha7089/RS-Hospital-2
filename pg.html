PATIENT GUIDE

Appointment

For appointments, call between 9:00 am to 4:00 pm (Mon–Sat except holidays)
Phone: +91.33.4033 3333
Email: gre@peerlesshospital.com

Admission Information

There are three categories of patients who may require hospital admission:
- Patients referred by Emergency Department for emergency admission
- Patients referred from Doctors of TPA / Corporate
- Patients referred by the Consultants for Planned admission

The patient or relatives will be required to fill in the Admission form available in the admission counter (next to Emergency Department). After registration for admission, two visitors’ passes will be issued. The accepted mode of payment is by Cash/Demand Draft/Credit Card/Debit Card. Patients can be admitted round the clock on all 365 days.

Visiting Hours

Visiting hours: 10:30 am to 11:30 am and 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm
Children below 12 years are allowed only on Sundays and Holidays between 4:00 pm and 6:00 pm. They are not allowed in any of the Intensive Care Units. For patient admission in ITU, ICCU, CTVS/ITU, NICU, visitors are allowed with restricted entry from 9:00 am to 9:30 am and 4:00 pm to 4:30 pm.
